# 🎯 FIGMA PROMPT
## Island Hopping Reflection Exercise: Mapping Progress vs Stagnation Towards Team Outcomes

Design a collaborative FigJam/Figma workshop board using an **Island Hopping Journey** metaphor to help teams reflect on their progress towards achieving team outcomes and identify actions for improvement.

---

# 🏝️ OVERALL CONCEPT

Create a large ocean-themed workspace with four islands connected by dotted pathways.

```text
🏝️ WHAT ISLAND
      ↓
🏝️ HOW ISLAND
      ↓
🏝️ SO WHAT ISLE
      ↓
🏝️ NOW WHAT CAY
```

The exercise should enable teams to move through a structured reflection journey using the **STOP – START – CONTINUE** framework.

---

# 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Support teams to:

- Assess current progress towards team outcomes.
- Identify areas of stagnation and inefficiency.
- Recognise strengths and successful practices.
- Explore opportunities for innovation and improvement.
- Develop practical actions aligned with team outcome areas.
- Create a shared understanding of what needs to stop, start and continue.

---

# 🏝️ WHAT ISLAND
## "Where are we right now?"

### Objective
Create a shared understanding of the team's current position against their intended outcomes.

### STOP

Prompt teams to consider:

- What activities should we stop doing?
- What processes or practices are slowing us down?
- What outdated approaches no longer add value?
- Where are the bottlenecks?
- What duplication of effort exists?
- What causes confusion, delays or rework?
- What distracts us from achieving our outcomes?

### START

Prompt teams to consider:

- What should we start doing?
- What opportunities are we not taking advantage of?
- What innovation should we trial?
- What new habits would improve our performance?
- What tools or systems could add value?
- How can we work smarter?
- What would accelerate achievement of our outcomes?

### CONTINUE

Prompt teams to consider:

- What is working well?
- Which practices consistently deliver results?
- What strengths contribute to success?
- What should be maintained?
- What should be protected and scaled?
- What already supports achievement of our outcomes?

---

# 🏝️ HOW ISLAND
## "How did we get here?"

### Objective
Explore behaviours, systems, processes and ways of working that influence progress.

### STOP

- What behaviours contribute to delays?
- What habits limit collaboration?
- What processes create unnecessary complexity?
- What creates frustration for stakeholders?

### START

- How can we improve communication?
- What new ways of working should we experiment with?
- How can technology better support delivery?
- What partnerships should we strengthen?
- What capabilities should we build?

### CONTINUE

- What collaboration practices are effective?
- What leadership behaviours support success?
- What routines help maintain focus?
- What team habits contribute to consistent delivery?

---

# 🏝️ SO WHAT ISLE
## "What does this mean?"

### Objective
Understand the impact of current practices on team outcomes.

### STOP

- What risks exist if we continue current inefficiencies?
- What are the consequences of not changing?
- Which outcomes could be negatively impacted?
- What stakeholder impacts could result?

### START

- What opportunities could be unlocked through improvement?
- What future benefits could emerge?
- What value could be created?
- What would success look like?

### CONTINUE

- What positive outcomes are currently being achieved?
- What strengths are directly contributing to success?
- Which practices should be protected?
- What successful approaches should become standard practice?

---

# 🏝️ NOW WHAT CAY
## "Where do we go next?"

### Objective
Transform reflection into actionable commitments.

### STOP

- What will we intentionally stop doing?
- What process should be simplified or removed?
- What can be eliminated immediately?

### START

- What new actions should be implemented?
- What improvements should be trialled?
- What opportunities should we pursue?
- What capability should be strengthened?

### CONTINUE

- What successful practices should be maintained?
- What strengths should be scaled?
- What should become embedded in how we work?
